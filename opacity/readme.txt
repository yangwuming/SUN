Subroutines mainopac.f, alexop.f, and opal95.f are used to read low and high
temperature opacity tables and output opacity. Old version subroutines interpolated
between a given Z1 and Z2 for Z-diffusion. These news subroutines can automaticly 
choose Zi determined by zpa(i).
